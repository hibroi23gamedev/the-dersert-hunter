About

The Dersert Hunter is a game where you are trapped inside the Dersert and you
need to find a way out and beat the boss, there are lots of things that are
blocking your adventure, like thrist and hunger.

Stuff in it:
Player - The Player is just a normal person, can drink, eat, poop, and just do stuff.
Thrist - The thrist slowly runs out, so you have to drink or do other stuff to get you Thrist up!
